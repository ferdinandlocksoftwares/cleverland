OUR PROGRAMS

English & Chinese Curriculum

Baby Literacy

9–18 months old

Encourages babies cognitively through routine, movement, language, and social-emotional discovery.

Pretoddler

18–24 months old

An interactive experience that supports cognition, language, self-help skills, and emotional development.

Toddler

2–3 years old

Builds independence while helping children explore their world and transition smoothly into school life.

Nursery

3–4 years old

Combines repetition, practice, and educational play to prepare young learners for academic schooling.

Pre-Kinder

4 years old

Focuses on literacy, foundational math and science, plus patience, sharing, and self-concept.

Kinder

5 years old

Expands vocabulary, science, social, and math skills with hands-on activities and motor development.

Mission

To nurture curiosity, confidence, and character in every child through engaging, age-appropriate learning experiences.

Vision

To shape joyful young learners who are prepared socially, emotionally, and academically for lifelong success.

Philosophy

Learning through play, guided discovery, and a supportive atmosphere where each child’s unique strengths are valued.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Our goal is to immerse a child's intelligence and talent in a real-world setting.

✔ We report each child's academic and socio-emotional developments.
✔ We offer fun and educational field trips and presentations.
✔ We cultivate small-class settings for individual attention.
✔ We value each child’s unique mind.
✔ We provide academic and after-school support.
✔ We make learning exciting and memorable.
